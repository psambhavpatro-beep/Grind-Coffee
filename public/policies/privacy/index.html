Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, and how we protect it — in compliance with India's Information Technology Act and the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023.

Section 1 — What We Collect

We collect: your name, phone number, delivery address, email address, order history, and payment confirmation details. We do not store card numbers or CVVs; payment data is handled entirely by Razorpay.

Section 2 — Why We Collect It

We use your data to: process and deliver your orders, communicate order status, improve our platform and product selection, and send you updates if you opt in.

Section 3 — Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties except as required to fulfill your order (e.g., delivery partners) or comply with applicable law.

Section 4 — Cookies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to understand how you use our platform. This may include analytics tools and advertising pixels. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though some features may not function correctly.

Section 5 — Your Rights

Under applicable Indian law, you have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal data. To exercise these rights, contact us at support@thatsgrind.com.

Section 6 — Third Party Services

Our platform uses Razorpay for payments and may use third-party analytics tools. These services have their own privacy policies which we encourage you to review.

Section 7 — Changes

We may update this policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes via the platform or email.
