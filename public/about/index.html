Our Story

Built out of a genuine problem

Grind exists because great coffee shouldn't come with a three-day wait. We're Bangalore's specialty coffee quick commerce app — delivered in 45 minutes, no compromises on quality.

The Origin

How Grind started

It started with brain fog and a forgotten coffee order. Sambhav, our founder, was running on empty — not because good coffee didn't exist in Bangalore, but because getting it delivered fast felt impossible. He was maintaining a Notion tracker just to remember when to reorder. He'd still forget. And when he did order, it took days.

The existing options were either too slow, too generic, or both. Specialty coffee — the kind that actually has a story behind the bean — was treated like a slow-moving luxury product when it didn't have to be.

So Sambhav built Grind. The idea was simple: treat specialty coffee the way you'd treat any other essential. Make it fast. Keep it good. Don't make people think about it — just get it to them.

What started as a personal frustration is now a quick commerce platform serving coffee lovers across all of Bangalore. Every order is a small proof that the wait wasn't worth it.

What drives us

Three things we don't compromise on

Speed is the product

Quick commerce isn't a feature — it's the entire point. If you have to plan your coffee order days in advance, something is broken.

Specialty, not generic

We only carry coffees that have something interesting to say — single origins, thoughtful roasts, beans with provenance. No filler.

Built to experiment

Sambhav describes himself as a nerd who loves to experiment. That means we're always testing new origins, formats, and ideas — if it's good, it makes the cut.
